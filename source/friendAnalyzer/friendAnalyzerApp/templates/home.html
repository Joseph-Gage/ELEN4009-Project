The Real Time Video Coding Dashboard

This Dashboard contains visualisations of the data captured from the Real Time Video Coding Platform.

Prime Time & Location Analysis

This visualization view is comprized of a Day/Hour Heat Map that shows the prime viewing hours during the week filtered by each channel. It also has a Global Geographical Heat Map that shows the prime viewing areas around the world.

Network & Channel Comparison

This view illustrates the channel network traffic. This information can then be filtered by each channel and also by the hour of the day, day of the week or month of the year. These visuals also demostrate the locations of the global network traffic.

TV Programme Analysis

This view visualizes the popularity of each TV programme hosted on the platform. This view includes a time series chart that shows the viewership of a selected TV programme over time and a sortable bar chart that plots the viewership of each TV programme against each other. There is also a global geographical map that shows the locations in the world each TV programme has viewership in.
